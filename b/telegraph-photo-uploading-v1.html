Let's add a image to telegraph

05th Oct 2024

So here is a rough workaround for adding an image to Telegraph, using a website as an image hoster.

This is the website link that is in the middle of this whole case. Sign up if you want. It's not mandatory.

Now then, let's do it step-by-step.

  • Upload your image to the website. I'm not going to fucking tell you how to do it. Just basic shit.
  • Then, you'll get a section filled with various types of links. You only need the link under the title of Direct Link:
    Example : 
    https://i.postimg.cc/SsYLVgfW/IMG-20240912-014229-401.jpg
  • Now copy the link, go to your telegraph page, hit the edit button and set the coordinations, tap the <> icon, paste your link, and hit enter. (or ⤶ icon in phone keyboard)
  • If you didn't press the enter key after pasting the link, it won't do shit. Just hit the enter button.

And that's pretty much it. Adios...
