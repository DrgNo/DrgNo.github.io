About us

We are the Percy Jackson Fan Club, the largest and most vibrant community of demigods in sri lanka, exclusively on Telegram platform! Our positive and engaging user base is bound by a shared love for Rick Riordan's world of mythology and adventure.

Whether you're here to discuss the latest book theories, share fan art, or dive into all things related to Camp Half-Blood or greek mythology, you'll find like-minded fans who are as passionate as you are. Join us to celebrate the magic of Percy Jackson and create lasting memories with fellow fans!

Our core values

  • Safe Environment

    Respectful community, free from negativity, where all fans feel welcome.

  • Joyful Userbase

    Enthusiastic members sharing excitement, laughter, and a passion for the fandom.

  • Informative Posts

    Up-to-date articles, theories, and discussions to deepen your fandom knowledge.

  • Place for Creativity

    Share fan art, stories, and ideas; a hub for fan expression.

Channel History

Community Insight

  1. Poseidon into race

    27th of Jan, 2024

    Cabin Poseidon launched their first weekly game, diving into interactive adventures and setting the stage for weekly community fun.

  2. Weekly cabin games

    21st of Sep, 2023

    Weekly Cabin Games commenced, bringing interactive Percy Jackson adventures, fostering camaraderie and unleashing our inner demigods in thrilling community challenges.

  3. Thousand yard completed

    20th of Mar, 2023

    Reaching 1,000 subscribers marked a milestone, a testament to our growing community's passion and dedication to the Percy Jackson universe, fueling our journey onward.

  4. Channel Re-Roling

    2nd of Feb, 2022

    Faced with unavoidable technical hurdles, this channel emerged as a renewed sanctuary, dedicated to providing a seamless and enriched Percy Jackson experience for our devoted community.

  5. Start of the great adventure

    9th of Jul, 2021

    The channel's inception marked the beginning of our shared demigod journey. From its very first day, we began building our Percy Jackson community. The channel's creation ignited a spark, uniting fans of the PJO universe.
